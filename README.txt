How to Use
Home Page:
A list view of all your assignments due in the next 7 days.
Check the checkbox to mark an assignment complete

Courses Page:
Courses and Assignments can be added on this page.
Assignment name and due dates are required or else information would not be posted into the database.
Click on the trash can icon to delete a course or assignment.
